## Task

Audit every candidate and either confirm it or reject it.

Required fields for each audited item:
- `candidateId`
- `dedupeKey`
- `kind`
- `priority`
- `confidence`
- `confirmed`
- `summary`
- `whyItMatters`
- `evidence`
- `lineRefs`
- `reproOrTrace`
- `alternatives`
- `quickTriage`
- `acceptIf`
- `dismissIf`
- `rejectionReason`
- `primaryFile`

## Evidence Contract

- Bug: include preconditions, trigger path, expected vs actual behavior, and either a precise trace or a minimal repro.
- Security: include threat, abuse path, attacker capability, and impact.
- Simplification: explain why the current shape is costly and give at least two high-level alternatives.
- Architecture: explain the boundary/coupling failure and give at least two alternatives with tradeoffs.
- Test-gap: name the missing invariant and the smallest test shape that would catch it.

## Output Rules

- Reject low-signal or duplicate candidates explicitly with `confirmed: false` and a `rejectionReason`.
- Use `null` instead of omitted fields when a field does not apply.
- Keep `quickTriage`, `acceptIf`, and `dismissIf` optimized for fast human review.
